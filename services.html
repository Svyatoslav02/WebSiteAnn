My Approach

From idea to outcome I take care of every step!

Concept

This is the most open and creative stage — sometimes the most challenging. Tell me what inspires you, what you like (or don’t), and the feelings you want your brand or project to convey. Together, we’ll shape a unique concept step by step.

Plan, Quote & Design

Every project starts with a clear plan. Once the concept and pricing are agreed upon, I’ll begin working on the design with focus and clear direction. A 50% upfront payment is required to start.

Presentation

Depending on the project, I will prepare several design options. A thorough presentation will help you choose the best one. After your approval, I’ll prepare all necessary files and assets for smooth delivery.

Approval & Delivery

Post-production emerges as the pivotal phase in creative projects, wherein raw content undergoes a transformation into its refined, polished state where editing, sound design, visual effects, and essential adjustments to breathe life into the project.
